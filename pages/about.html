Currently a Ph.D. candidate, I am working towards a degree in Bioinformatics and Computation Biology at Iowa State University in Ames, Iowa. My current plan is to graduate this summer, in 2021, and begin a new adventure outside of the graduate student life..

A member of the GERMS lab, in the department of Agricultural and Biosystems Engineering, our research focuses on environmental microbiomes and metagenomes; predominantly soil, but also water-systems, manure, plant-interactions, and even flower nectar.

My research focuses heavily on data analysis and building tools and pipelines for reproducible research. I enjoy data exploration and answering questions through data, but I equally enjoy enabling other researchers to answer their own questions with useful tools. This is why I put together tools and packages such as the phylosmith package, along with others you may find on my GitHub.
