I am currently a Ph.D. candidate, working towards a degree in Bioinformatics and Computation Biology at Iowa State University in Ames, Iowa. My current plan is to graduate this summer and begin a new adventure outside of the graduate student life..

A member of the GERMS lab , in the department of Agriculture and Biosystems Engineering , our research focuses on environmental microbiomes and metagenomes; predominantly soil, but also water-systems, manure, plant-interactions, and even flower nectar.

My research focuses heavily on data analysis and building tools for reproducible research. I enjoy data exploration and answering questions through data, but I equally enjoy enabling other researchers to answer their own questions with useful tools. This is why I put together tools and packages such as the phylosmith package, along with others you may find on my GitHub.

Outisde of research, I have also been fortunate to travel to various parts of the world. Several were on birding trips with my father, but also some for conferences, and others just for vacation. I am not a photography enthusiast, but I like to document the places I have been. The photos page I have here has been more of a fun task in learning HTML, and a place to put photos to show family, than anything else. If I did cool wet-lab projects then I could probably put some neat science pictures up.. but in my case, that's pretty much just my Github page .
